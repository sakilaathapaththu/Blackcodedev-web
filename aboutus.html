About BlackCode

Our Story

BlackCode began in 2020 as a group of passionate undergraduates taking on university assignment projects. What started with simple web and mobile application builds quickly evolved into a mission to deliver real-world, production-grade solutions.

With over 100 academic and freelance projects under our belt, our team gained valuable experience working across diverse platforms and stacks. This hands-on foundation laid the path to developing high-impact applications like the ePaddy Mobile App for smart agriculture, a full-featured Learning Management System (LMS), and the official website for Lexicon International School.

From solving student needs to serving businesses and institutions, BlackCode has become a trusted software partner delivering quality, reliability, and innovation. Our story is proof that even small beginnings can grow into something extraordinary.
